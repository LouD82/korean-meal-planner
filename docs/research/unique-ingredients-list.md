# Comprehensive List of Unique Ingredients for Korean Recipes

This document contains a comprehensive list of all unique ingredients required across the 10 documented Korean recipes in our meal planner project. The ingredients are organized by category for easier reference.

## Proteins
- Chicken thighs (boneless, skinless)
- Ground beef
- Ground pork
- Pork belly/shoulder
- Beef brisket
- Tofu (firm and soft)
- Eggs
- Fish cakes (eomuk)

## Vegetables and Produce
- Spinach
- Carrots
- Zucchini
- Bean sprouts
- Shiitake mushrooms (dried and fresh)
- Button mushrooms
- Oyster mushrooms
- Cucumber
- Onion (yellow and white)
- Green onions/scallions
- Garlic
- Ginger
- Korean radish (mu)
- Potato
- Cabbage
- Red bell pepper
- Green chili peppers
- Red chili peppers
- Fernbrake/Gosari (dried bracken fern)

## Noodles and Rice
- Sweet potato starch noodles (dangmyeon)
- Rice cakes (tteok, cylindrical type)
- Short-grain white rice
- Brown rice
- Jasmine rice

## Korean Pantry Staples
- Gochujang (Korean red pepper paste)
- Gochugaru (Korean red pepper flakes)
- Doenjang (Korean soybean paste)
- Kimchi (well-fermented)
- Soy sauce
- Sesame oil
- Rice vinegar
- Mirin
- Oyster sauce

## Other Pantry Items
- Milk
- Salt
- Sugar
- Brown sugar
- Corn syrup
- Potato starch
- Vegetable/olive oil
- Sesame seeds
- Vinegar (white/distilled)
- Black pepper
- MSG (optional)
- Sriracha
- Dumpling wrappers

## Optional Garnishes
- Peanuts (chopped)
- Kimchi (as a side/topping)

This list will be used as the basis for researching ingredient availability at H-Mart, identifying potentially hard-to-find ingredients, and creating a comprehensive shopping guide.
