# H-Mart Ingredient Availability Research

This document contains research findings on the availability of ingredients at H-Mart for the Korean recipes documented in our meal planner project. The information is organized by category to help shoppers efficiently locate ingredients.

## H-Mart Store Layout Overview

H-Mart is a Korean/Asian supermarket chain with stores across the United States. Most H-Mart locations follow a similar layout with these main departments:

- Produce section (fruits and vegetables)
- Meat and poultry department
- Seafood department (often with live seafood options)
- Frozen food section
- Refrigerated section (including tofu, kimchi, and other prepared foods)
- Dry goods and pantry staples
- Snacks and beverages
- Housewares section (cookware, utensils, etc.)
- Food court (in larger locations)

## Seasonal Availability Considerations

Some ingredients at H-Mart may have seasonal availability:
- Korean zucchini and certain peppers are more readily available in summer months
- Fresh Korean vegetables like perilla leaves may be seasonal
- Certain fruits used in Korean cooking (like Korean pears) are seasonal

## Common Brands to Look For

- CJ Foods: Popular brand for gochujang, doenjang, and other Korean sauces
- Chung Jung One: Well-known for various Korean condiments
- Wang Korea: Known for their red pepper powder
- Sempio: Popular brand for soy sauce and other condiments
- Ottogi: Makes various Korean food products
- Pulmuone: Known for tofu and fresh food products
