# Session 5: H-Mart Ingredient Availability Research

## Session Overview

In this session, we conducted comprehensive research on ingredient availability at H-Mart for Korean recipes. The goal was to create detailed documentation about where to find Korean ingredients at H-Mart, identify potentially hard-to-find ingredients, suggest suitable substitutes, and provide a practical shopping guide to help users efficiently navigate H-Mart stores.

## Research Methodology

Our research approach included:

1. **Ingredient Extraction**: We extracted and compiled a comprehensive list of unique ingredients from all 10 Korean recipes documented in previous sessions (5 lunch recipes and 5 dinner recipes).

2. **H-Mart Research**: We researched H-Mart's store layout, departments, and typical ingredient availability through:
   - H-Mart's official website
   - Chef interviews and shopping guides
   - Online resources about Korean grocery shopping
   - Articles about H-Mart store organization

3. **Hard-to-Find Ingredients Research**: We identified potentially challenging ingredients to find and researched suitable substitutes that would maintain authentic flavors.

4. **Shopping Guide Development**: We compiled practical tips for navigating H-Mart efficiently, finding specialty ingredients, and shopping economically.

## Key Findings

### H-Mart Ingredient Availability

- H-Mart carries the vast majority of ingredients needed for authentic Korean cooking
- The store is organized into clear departments: produce, meat, seafood, frozen, refrigerated, dry goods, and housewares
- Most Korean pantry staples (gochujang, doenjang, gochugaru, etc.) are readily available in the sauce and spice aisles
- Fresh Korean vegetables may have seasonal availability
- Pre-marinated meats for Korean BBQ are widely available in the meat department
- Multiple varieties of kimchi and banchan are available in refrigerated sections

### Hard-to-Find Ingredients

- Some specialty ingredients like perilla leaves (kkaennip), fernbrake (gosari), and certain seasonal items may be harder to find
- Korean soup soy sauce (guk-ganjang) might be unfamiliar to new shoppers
- We identified suitable substitutes for all potentially hard-to-find ingredients
- Many ingredients have alternative names or packaging that might confuse first-time shoppers

### Shopping Guide Insights

- H-Mart store layout is consistent across most locations, making the guide widely applicable
- Staff can be helpful resources for locating specific items
- Economical shopping opportunities include bulk buying, house brands, and loyalty programs
- Weekday mornings tend to be less crowded for shopping
- Food courts in larger H-Mart locations provide opportunities to try dishes before buying ingredients

## Documentation Created

This session resulted in the creation of several detailed documents:

1. **hmart-ingredient-availability.md**: A comprehensive guide to ingredient availability at H-Mart, organized by category (pantry staples, produce, meat/seafood, noodles/rice, specialty ingredients).

2. **hard-to-find-ingredients-substitutes.md**: Detailed information about potentially hard-to-find Korean ingredients, suitable substitutes, alternative names, and storage considerations.

3. **hmart-shopping-guide.md**: A practical guide to navigating H-Mart stores efficiently, including store layout, department information, shopping tips, and economical shopping strategies.

4. **unique-ingredients-list.md**: A compiled list of all unique ingredients extracted from the 10 Korean recipes documented in previous sessions.

## Conclusion

This research provides a comprehensive resource for Korean cooking enthusiasts who shop at H-Mart. The documentation will help users confidently navigate H-Mart stores, find specific ingredients for Korean recipes, identify suitable substitutes when needed, and shop efficiently and economically.

The findings confirm that H-Mart is an excellent one-stop shop for Korean cooking ingredients, with most items readily available. For the few potentially hard-to-find ingredients, we've provided practical substitutes that maintain authentic flavors while being more accessible.
